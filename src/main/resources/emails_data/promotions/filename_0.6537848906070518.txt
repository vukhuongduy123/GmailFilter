 UPGRADE YOUR CAREER WITH NAB, DON'T MISS THIS GREAT OPPORTUNITY

 
Do you want to work in a large-scale organization with a lot of experience?
Do you want the opportunity to develop your career under strong support from the company?
 
Let's become a ServiceNow Engineer at NAB Innovation Centre Vietnam.
 
NAB is finding a candidate accountable for the operational capability and the project uplifts to keep the platform current and to integrate other technologies into the platform.
 
With responsibilities:


	Work closely with developers, product and process owners to meet NAB's evolving requirements
	Implement and maintain integrations in ServiceNow to support NAB's “Always On” strategy
	Maintain and enhance the service catalog and service portal
	Support an ongoing process of improvement and consolidation
	Delivery of sustainable improvement solutions and benefits for our customers
	Consistent application of approach aligned to the enterprise frameworks and methodology


 
To be a part of NAB Innovation Centre Vietnam, you need to meet those requirements:


	Current ServiceNow certification
	Sound knowledge of delivery methodologies and associated tools, with working experience in Agile delivery
	Competent stakeholder management skills, with the ability to apply the fundamentals of Change Management throughout the delivery lifecycle
	Effective communication skills with the ability to synthesize data and communicate findings in a meaningful way
	Demonstrated application of Risk Management fundamentals
	Experience in working in a cloud-enabled environment
	Certification in AWS or Azure is desirable
